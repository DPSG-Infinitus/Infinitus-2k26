Battlegrounds of Cognition

Online Classes VI-VII

Redstone Rebellion

A Minecraft creative building competition where young engineers design and construct an innovative virtual world. Build worlds based on our theme "Cognition: Ghost in the Machine" live under strict time limits.

  • Composition: Max 2 per team
  • Platform: Java / Bedrock Edition
  • Duration: 1.5 Hours live build
  • Coordinator: Aadhya (+91 96671 67366)

Online Classes IV-V

Sentient Script

A digital cinematic and animation competition. Script, design, and render an original Scratch-based video visually exploring the transition of cold, linear code awakening into conscious self-aware thought.

  • Composition: Max 2 per team
  • Platform: Scratch exclusively
  • Submission: MP4 Export by 20th July
  • Coordinator: Sameer (+91 88265 59665)
Download Rulebook Register
Offline Classes VII-XII

Cortex

Showcase functional working tech prototypes (software, hardware, or AI) that solve real-world problems. This is a live demonstration of working systems. Pitch your engineering creations to a panel of expert judges.

  • Composition: 2 to 4 per team
  • Pitch Time: 4 Mins Pitch + 2 Mins Q&A
  • Mandatory: Physical prototype
  • Coordinator: Parth Garg (+91 97163 61010)
Download Rulebook Register
Offline Classes VIII-XII

Quantum Clash

A high-octane robocar combat tournament where custom-built engineered rovers clash in a closed arena. Battle to immobilize your opponents under strict weight, time, and weapons constraints.

  • Composition: 2 to 3 per team
  • Weight Limit: 5 kg Max (±5%)
  • Control Type: Wireless Remote Control Only
  • Coordinator: Yojan (+91 81787 34345)
Download Rulebook Register
Offline Classes VIII-XII

The Nexus Node

A frontend web development and UI/UX design competition. Design and code a responsive, multi-page website representing the definite interface for an awakened, self-aware cybernetic neural network.

  • Composition: 1 team of 2 per school
  • Requirements: HTML/CSS/JS or Frameworks
  • Scope: Min 4 functional pages
  • Coordinator: Dhruv (+91 77038 16431)

Offline Classes IX-XII

PyQuest

A Python-focused competitive coding tournament. Solves real-world algorithmic problems under strict time constraints. Showcase your programming efficiency, execution correctness, and data structure speed.

  • Composition: 1 team of 2 per school
  • Language: Python ONLY
  • Environment: Standard Offline IDEs
